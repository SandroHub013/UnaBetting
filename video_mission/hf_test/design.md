# UnaBetting — Video Design System

Cinematic, dark, editorial-luxury. The release-teaser register: restraint, big
kinetic type, one accent that does the talking.

## Palette
- `--ink` #0B0B0E — background (deep space)
- `--ink2` #15141A — panels
- `--bone` #EFE7D2 — primary text
- `--bone-dim` #A8A294 — secondary text
- `--clay` #C75A2A — accent (tennis clay)
- `--gold` #E69A10 — highlight (sparingly, the "hero" word)
- `--grass` #5BD08B — positive only

## Typography
- Display: 'Fraunces' (light/300, italic for accents)
- UI/labels: 'Archivo' (300–600), all-caps + tracking for kickers
- Mono/meta: 'JetBrains Mono'

## Motion
- Eases: power3.out / expo.out for entrances; power2.inOut for camera moves.
- Kinetic type rises from masked lines. Numbers count up.
- Ken Burns on stills (scale 1.0→1.10, slow).
- Transitions: crossfade / clip-reveal; never jump-cut.

## Don'ts
- No stock-y blue. No full-screen linear gradients (banding). No emoji.
- Don't promise winnings. The honest gap (66.3 vs 67.7) is the hook, not hidden.
